Kayaking and Swimming: The park is a year round day use park highlighted by a supervised sandy beach with a roped off swimming area and good choice for kayaking.

Biking, Walking, Sightseeing, and Dog Walking: A good choice to go for Biking, walking, sightseeing, and dog walking.

Picknicking: It has historic bridge and a good selection of paved trails and picnic sites. Located throughout the Boulevard Lake Park are numerous picnic sites. Each providing different views and amenities. All provide picnic tables and sightseeing benches. The park is also the site for festivals and events during the year. Check your calendar for dates.
