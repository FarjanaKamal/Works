Frequent Question Answers

1)What is Tbay Travels
Tbay Travels is a website created by students of Confederation College to provide information of nearby restaurants and places to the local communities of Thunder Bay

2) Who can use Tbay Travels
All the people of Thunder Bay can use the website

3) What would be done on the webiste
People can view the restaurant and places nearby. They can also see what activities could be done there

4) Can user add rating and reviews
Users can add rating,reviews and also can share the photos of places they visited
